01 — About Sealink

Marine technology, engineered for operational reality

Sealink Electric and Software Pvt Ltd develops software and digital systems for ships and maritime operators. Our work combines machine learning, naval architecture physics, and live operational data to help crews sail safer, cleaner, and more efficiently.

We are building AFCOS — an AI-Based Fuel Consumption Optimization System — with applications across Indian Coast Guard operations, commercial tankers, and future multi-vessel fleet deployments.

Mission

Deliver indigenous maritime AI that crews can trust — transparent, override-safe, and validated against real sea trials.

Focus areas

  • Engine performance monitoring
  • Weather-aware routing intelligence
  • CII / compliance support

02 — Flagship product

03 — Technology stack

Physics + data, not black-box guesses

AFCOS is built on Holtrop-Mennen resistance theory, MAN B&W engine physics, gradient-boosted fuel models, and open marine weather APIs — validated on 294+ operational rows from MT Magenta Ray and expanding multi-vessel datasets.
